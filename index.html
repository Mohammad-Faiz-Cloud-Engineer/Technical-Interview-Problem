Repository Structure

Temporal Organization

Problems categorized by recency: 30 days, 3 months, 6 months, and historical data for strategic preparation.

Structured Data

Each company directory contains five distinct CSV files for comprehensive coverage and flexible study approaches.

Regular Updates

Systematically maintained to reflect current hiring practices and interview patterns across the industry.

Research-Driven Approach

The problem sets are compiled through systematic analysis of interview reports, online coding platforms, and publicly available interview experiences. Each entry is verified and categorized to ensure accuracy and relevance.

This temporal categorization allows candidates to prioritize recent interview patterns while maintaining access to comprehensive historical data.
